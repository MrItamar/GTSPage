Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Guess The Song (GTS) is a music-based quiz game designed for entertainment. Your privacy is important to us, and this Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the game and its related support services.

Overview

Guess The Song does not require user accounts and does not ask for personal identification in order to play. Most of the game can be used without providing any personal information.

Information We Collect

The game itself does not collect personal data such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Physical location
  • Contacts or photos

If you choose to interact with the public support page linked from the app, the following information may be collected:

  • The text content of comments or replies you submit
  • The date and time the comment was posted

Support comments are public and visible to other users. Please do not include personal, sensitive, or identifying information in comments.

How Information Is Used

Information collected through the support page is used only for:

  • Displaying public support discussions
  • Responding to issues or feedback related to the game
  • Improving gameplay experience and stability

Guess The Song does not use collected information for advertising, marketing, or user profiling.

Data Storage and Services

Public support comments are stored using Firebase Firestore, a cloud database service provided by Google.

No personal user accounts are created, and no private user profiles are maintained.

Third-Party Services

The app may rely on third-party services (such as music APIs or game infrastructure) strictly to enable core gameplay features. These services are not used for tracking individual users.

Children’s Privacy

Guess The Song is intended for a general audience and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If personal information is discovered in public support comments, it may be removed.

Your Choices

You are not required to submit comments or feedback to use the app. Participation in public support discussions is optional.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated to reflect changes to the game or its services. Any updates will be published on this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or the Guess The Song app, please use the support page linked within the app.
